==========================================================================================
---------------------------------------------
Limitations

These are strict rules that aims to balance the
overall gameplay experience. 
----------------------------------------------

Hand Limit:
	Hand size limit for each player is 4. Extra cards are discarded to the "Discard Pile"

Discard Pile Limit:
	The size of the discard pile is 10. The discard pile is removed from play after it hits it's limit.
	Then a new pile is created.

Double Rules: 
	Max double rolls = 2
	Max movement after double = 2

Dice Card Effects:
	When dice cards are played, only a single die must be used to carry out the 
	effects. 
	For example --
		Dice-Appear!
			Roll a die    // Only a single die must be rolled.
